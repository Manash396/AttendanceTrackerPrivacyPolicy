Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18/11/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Attendance Tracker App ("we", "our", "the App") handles your data. We value your privacy and ensure that the App works completely offline.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information unless the user manually enters it. Information entered may include:

  • Attendance records (dates, statuses)

All data is stored locally on your device.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only for marking and managing attendance. It is not uploaded, processed, or transmitted outside your device.

3. Data Storage & Security

The App operates fully offline. No data leaves your device. You are responsible for exporting or backing up your data if needed.

4. No Internet, No Tracking

  • No online servers
  • No tracking
  • No cookies
  • No analytics
  • No advertisements

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your data with any third party.

6. Permissions Used

No permissions are used for data collection.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not target children. If used in schools, data stays offline on the educator’s device.

8. Your Data Control

  • You can delete attendance data anytime.
  • Uninstalling the App deletes all locally stored data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be posted on this page.
